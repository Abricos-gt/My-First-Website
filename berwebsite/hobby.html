Hobbies

Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy a variety of activities that help me stay creative and balanced:

  • Tech Exploration: I enjoy experimenting with new programming languages, building small projects, and contributing to open-source initiatives.
  • Photography: I love capturing moments of everyday life through photography. It's a creative outlet for me that allows me to pay attention to the finer details.
  • Outdoor Activities: I enjoy hiking and cycling as a way to stay active and recharge my mind.
  • Volunteering: I am passionate about mentoring young women in STEM, and I have volunteered at various events that encourage girls to pursue careers in technology.

These hobbies not only help me unwind but also complement my career goals by fostering creativity, problem-solving, and leadership skills.
